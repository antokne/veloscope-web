A simple app connecting to radar to record and view traffic data while riding or analysing post-ride.

Veloscope enables cyclists to connect with compatible radars via Bluetooth to monitor approaching vehicle traffic in real-time or review data afterward through MyBikeTraffic.com.

Features

  • Radar Traffic View — Customizable positioning (left/right) with optional audible alerts as vehicles approach
  • Vehicle Monitoring — Real-time vehicle counts plus totals during activity sessions
  • Speed Data — Displays relative approach speeds and absolute speeds of nearest vehicles
  • Activity Recording — iPhone GPS captures start time, duration, distance, average/max speeds with integrated radar data
  • Auto Pause/Resume — Configurable automatic pausing functionality
  • Activity Recovery — Restart capability without losing session data
  • Auto-Upload — Optional background uploading to MyBikeTraffic.com with success notifications
  • Dark/Light Mode — Full theme compatibility
  • FIT File Export — Share activity files with other apps or computers
  • Data Field Customization — Drag-and-drop reordering interface
  • Radar Disconnect Alerts — Optional notifications when connection is lost

Compatible Radars

  • Garmin Varia RTL515
  • Magene L508

Pricing

Free to try with 3 activities. Unlock unlimited recording with a one-time in-app purchase.
