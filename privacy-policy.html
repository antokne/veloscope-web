Privacy Policy

Personal info

Veloscope does not collect any personal data.

How we collect info

Veloscope uses GPS to record your location and speed. It also records traffic data such as approach speed and range from a paired radar. These are recorded into an activity FIT file.

This FIT file is kept locally and can be uploaded to MyBikeTraffic.com if you choose to upload it.

How we use your info

Your recorded activities are not used in any way.

When we share your info

Your recorded activities are only shared to MyBikeTraffic.com if you configure Veloscope to do so.

How we store your info

Your recorded activities are stored in the application documents folder and, if you choose, may be backed up to iCloud.
